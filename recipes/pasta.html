Pasta

Description

A simple homemade pasta for you and your family!

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

How to prepare

  1. Make the dough
  2. Turn the dough on and knead it for 10 minutes
  3. Let it rest
  4. Divide the dough into balls
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes
