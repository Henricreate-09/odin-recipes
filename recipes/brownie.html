Brownie

Description

A brownie is a simple cake you can make at home!!!

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted margarine
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 2 pinches of salt
  • 1 bar of semi-sweet chocolate, chopped in cubes
  • 3/4 cup of chocolate drink mix
  • 1 and 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract or essence
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chopped nuts

How to prepare

  1. Mix the eggs and sugar
  2. Then, gather all the other ingredients and mix everything to form a uniform cream
  3. Pour into a baking sheet lined with paper-manteed
  4. It'll be ready when the top is stained and if, when pricked with something, be moist(due to the melted chocolated)
  5. Chop it in squares while warm and enjoy
  6. Mix the eggs and sugar
